Birney – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Overview

Introduction

Welcome to Birney, a personal finance tracking application developed by Pakphum Ben ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Birney mobile application. Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not access the application.

Data Collection

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Birney:

  • Account Information: Name and email address obtained through Google Sign-In.
  • Financial Data: Transactions, budgets, wallet balances, and spending categories that you voluntarily enter into the app.
  • Images: Photos you choose to upload or capture using your camera (e.g., for receipts or profile pictures).
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers used for authentication and app stability.
  • Usage Data: Interactions within the app (screens visited, features used) to improve user experience.
How We Use It

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Birney account.
  • Provide, operate, and maintain the money-tracking features of the app.
  • Sync your financial data securely across sessions.
  • Send you important notifications related to your account or budgets (if enabled).
  • Improve, personalise, and expand the app's functionality.
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud.
Third Parties

Third-Party Services

Birney integrates the following third-party services that may collect information under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase / Firebase Authentication – identity management and secure authentication.
  • Google Sign-In – OAuth-based login using your Google account.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

Permissions

App Permissions

Birney requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera & Photo Library: To allow you to attach receipt images or set a profile photo. Images are only uploaded when you explicitly choose to do so.
  • Internet Access: Required to sync your data and authenticate your account.
Data Storage

Data Storage & Security

Your financial data is stored on our secure backend servers and protected using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS). Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted secure storage. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Correction: You may view and update your personal information within the app at any time.
  • Data Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.
  • Withdraw Consent: You may revoke Google Sign-In permissions through your Google account settings at any time.
Children

Children's Privacy

Birney is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Updates

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
