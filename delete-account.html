Delete Your Birney Account

We're sorry to see you go. Your data will be permanently removed.

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile (name, email)
  • All transactions, budgets, and wallet data
  • Uploaded images and receipts
  • All spending categories and settings
⚠️ This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to birdlabs.service@gmail.com with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include the email address associated with your Birney account (your Google account email).
  3. We will process your request within 7 business days and send a confirmation once your account has been deleted.
Send Deletion Request

Data Retention

After your account is deleted, all personal data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. Anonymised, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained for analytical purposes only.
